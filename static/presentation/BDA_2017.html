SISP Conference 2017, Urbino 14-16

The essentials

  • Co-founded in 2009 by Beppe Grillo, a comedian and political activist, and a web strategist Gianroberto Casaleggio
  • In 2013, second most-voted party for the Chamber of Deputies
  • Direct "internet" democracy, anti-establishment, environmentalism and euroscepticism

A new kind of opposition party

  1. post-ideological
  2. representing neglected or unheard voices
  3. refusing dialogue with other parties (except when it fits its agenda)

Data

  • Content coding of parliamentary activities in the Chamber of Deputies (1262 bills, 1065 oral questions and 1383 written questions)
  • 4 "main" opposition parties: 5SM, Forza Italia, Lega Nord, Sinistra Ecologia e Libertà
  • Letta and Renzi cabinets
  • One of the 230 policy codes (aggregated in 21 policy areas) making up the Italian Policy Agendas codebook (http://www.comparativeagendas.net/italy)

Preliminary conclusions

  • the 5SM profile cross-cuts the left-right axis
  • it differs only for a few issues from other opposition parties in the current legislature
  • both agenda-setter and agenda-taker - different relationship with different opposition parties
